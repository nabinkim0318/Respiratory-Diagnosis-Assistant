Here are some tips on how to use our application:

  • Use the search page to input your query or upload an audio file for analysis.
  • Click on the search button to initiate the query process.
  • Once the results are displayed, you can listen to audio recordings, view metadata, and explore visualizations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I upload an audio file?
A: On the search page, select the option to upload an audio file and choose the file from your device.
Q: How do I search patient cases?
A: You can click one of the diseases in the dropdown options.
